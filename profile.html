Kingswill

The 28-year-old Kingswill, whose real name is Joel Udochukwu Attama, is the first-ever signee of QFT Record Label. He is an exceptional singer and songwriter who hails from Orba Nsukka in Enugu State, Nigeria. He graduated from the University of Nigeria Nsukka, where he studied Medical Laboratory Science. Kingswill's passion for music has driven him to pursue his dream from a young age and he has been quite an impressive artiste. His extraordinary talent and dedication to his craft have earned him a place among the rising stars in the Nigerian music industry.

Having already collaborated with esteemed artistes such as Jeriq and Jaywiz (RollBoy), Kingswill is set to make a lasting impression with his upcoming immensely fascinating releases. His diverse musical influences and unique sound promise to captivate audiences both within Nigeria and beyond. With plans to collaborate with artists from around the world, Kingswill is poised to leave an uneraseable mark on the global music ecosystem.

With a strong focus on creating quality music and building a loyal fan base, Kingswill's partnership with QFT Record Label marks the beginning of an exciting journey towards success. While he continues to hone his craft and push boundaries in the industry, Kingswill is primed to become a household name in the music world. Keep an eye out for this talented artiste as he takes the music scene by storm. He heralds a new era of sonic innovation where his transformative vision, reassures towards redefinition of the very essence of contemporary Nigerian music.
